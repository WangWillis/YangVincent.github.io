About Delta Sigma Pi


Purpose:

Delta Sigma Pi is a professional fraternity organized to foster the study of business in universities; to encourage scholarship, social activity and the association of students for their mutual advancement by research and practice; to promote closer affiliation between the commercial world and students of commerce, and to further a higher standard of commercial ethics and culture and the civic and commercial welfare of the community.

Advice from Brothers

"I think the biggest tip that I can give about finding an internship is to be extremely persistent. Don't just limit yourself to a few applications; apply to everything you think you might have the slightest interest in and efinitely network and utilize your connections. Delta Sigma Pi taught me how to present myself professionally, and that carried from the moment I interiewed, to my daily presence at work and my presentation skills in front of VPs and Directors"


Brother Tiffany Lin | Finance Summer Analyst at Barclays Investment Bank

"The best advice I have is that you have to make sure that there are no errors in your resume, cover letter, presentations, and emails. I did not believe having attention-to-detail was this crucial, but after networking and interviewing with HP managers, every one of them emphasized the importance of being error-free. I can say that Delta Sigma Pi has definitely helped me in this aspect, and because of this skill, I've had the opportunity to present in front of eight executives and directors"


Brother Leslie Chan | Supply Chain Analyst Intern at Hewlett-Packard

"Remember that a job needs you more than you 'want' it. People tend to experience anxiety when they are in the position to 'need' something that is under the control of someone else. By having the frame of mind 'want' rather than 'need' the job, you will go into the interview with a level of confidence that you wouldn't have otherwise. Delta Sigma Pi has definitely helped with my confidence by providing me with a sense of belongingness. This feeling that people crave for can help many students discover the confidence they need to pursue the dreams they've always been afraid to chase"

Brother Henley Huang | Accounting Clerk at Hotel Nikko San Francisco
